ABOUT KRISHNA GARIGIPATI

KRISHNA GARIGIPATI is an Author, Screenplay writer, and Poet. A boy from Kakinada who started his writing journey just a year before the world immersed in the pandemic.

Krishna made a lot of circles around him, assisting the newbie screenwriters from WGA (Writers Guild Of America) and ISA (International Screenwriters Association) as a star reviewer in Coverfly. He was successful in helping screenwriters to analyze the strength of their scripts till date.

Apart from screenplay reviewing, Krishna Garigipati is an enthusiastic writer who shows massive interest in both Novel writing and Short Story writing.

KRISHNA GARIGIPATI is very interseted in CODING, DATA SCIENCE and AIML.
